Winner!

Winner is a 2017 Indian Telugu-language sports action comedy film jointly produced by Nallamalupu Bujji, Tagore Madhu on Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Productions & Leo Productions banner and directed by Gopichand Malineni. Starring Sai Dharam Tej, Rakul Preet Singh, Jagapati Babu in the lead roles and music composed by S. Thaman.[1] Production began in March 2016[2] and principal photography commenced in August 2016 in Hyderabad.[3] The film was released worldwide on 24 February 2017.
